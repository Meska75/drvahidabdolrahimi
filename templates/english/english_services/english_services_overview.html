Our Specialized Services

Dr. Abdolrahimi's clinic offers a full spectrum of diagnostic, therapeutic, and surgical eye care services, utilizing state-of-the-art equipment and the highest quality standards.

Clinic
DIAGNOSIS & TREATMENT

Office Services

Dr. Abdolrahimi's clinic is equipped with the most advanced diagnostic and therapeutic devices available today. Services include comprehensive eye examination, retinal OCT imaging, fluorescein angiography, intravitreal injection (Anti-VEGF), visual field testing (perimetry), and intraocular pressure measurement (tonometry) — all provided in a calm, organized, and fully professional environment.

Surgery
SPECIALIZED SURGERY

Surgical Services

With the most advanced surgical techniques and over 1,500+ successful operations, Dr. Abdolrahimi performs a wide range of specialized eye surgeries including vitrectomy (retina), cataract surgery, retinal laser, glaucoma surgery, SLT and YAG laser, and endotamponade. All procedures are carried out to the highest safety standards with comprehensive pre- and post-operative care.
