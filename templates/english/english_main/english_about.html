Biography

Ophthalmologist & Surgeon
Retina & Laser Sub-Specialist

Dr. Vahid Abdolrahimi is an ophthalmologist and surgeon with a sub-specialty in Retina and Laser, and a member of the European and American Societies of Ophthalmic Surgeons. With over 20 years of clinical experience, he delivers specialist services at the highest standards of quality to his patients.

Dr. Abdolrahimi's areas of expertise include all therapeutic and cosmetic ophthalmic surgical procedures (Phaco, LASIK, Strabismus, and Blepharoplasty), prevention and treatment of diabetic eye complications, and vision testing and eyeglass prescription services.

Specialties

Areas of Expertise

Dr. Abdolrahimi specializes in treating a wide spectrum of retinal diseases using the most advanced therapeutic methods

Age-Related Macular Degeneration (AMD)

Diagnosis and treatment of age-related macular degeneration using anti-VEGF injections and laser therapy

Diabetic Retinopathy

Prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of diabetic eye complications using focal laser and intravitreal injections

Retinal Detachment

Emergency and elective surgery for the repair of retinal detachment using modern techniques to restore vision

Intravitreal Injection

Anti-VEGF drug injections for the treatment of macular edema, wet AMD, and other related indications

OCT Imaging

Expert interpretation of next-generation OCT and OCT-Angiography for precise retinal disease diagnosis

Laser Therapy

Argon laser therapy and pan-retinal photocoagulation for various vascular retinal diseases
