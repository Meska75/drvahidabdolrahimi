SURGICAL SPECIALTIES

Specialized Eye Surgeries

A full range of specialized retina, vitreous, and other ocular surgeries using the latest techniques

Retinal Surgery (Vitrectomy)

Advanced vitreous surgery for treatment of retinal detachment and vitreous hemorrhage.

Cataract Surgery

Phacoemulsification with minimal incision and premium IOL implantation to restore clear vision.

Retinal Laser

Treatment of retinal tears and lesions with argon laser to protect and preserve vision.

Glaucoma Surgery

Trabeculectomy and aqueous drainage implant for long-term intraocular pressure control.

Glaucoma Laser (SLT / YAG)

Selective laser trabeculoplasty (SLT) and YAG iridotomy to reduce IOP without open surgery.

Endotamponade (Gas / Silicone)

Use of gas or silicone oil to stabilize the retina following vitrectomy.
