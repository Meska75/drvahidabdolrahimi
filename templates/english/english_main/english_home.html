About the Doctor

Over 20 Years of Expertise in Ophthalmic Surgery & Treatment

Dr. Vahid Abdolrahimi, with over 20 years of clinical experience, is one of the most distinguished ophthalmologists in Iran and a member of the esteemed European and American societies of ophthalmic surgeons. Utilizing the most advanced surgical techniques, he offers a broad spectrum of services — including retinal surgery, LASIK, Phaco, strabismus, and blepharoplasty — to the highest international standards.

  • Ophthalmologist & Retina / Laser Sub-Specialist
  • Member of European & American Societies of Ophthalmic Surgeons
  • Phaco, LASIK, Strabismus & Blepharoplasty — therapeutic and cosmetic surgery
  • Prevention & treatment of diabetic eye complications
  • Vision testing & eyeglass prescription
