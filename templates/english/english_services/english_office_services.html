OUR SERVICES

Specialized Clinic Services

A full range of diagnostic and therapeutic ophthalmology services using state-of-the-art equipment

Eye Examination & Consultation

Comprehensive eye assessment and individualized treatment planning for each patient.

OCT Imaging

Advanced cross-sectional retinal imaging for precise diagnosis of macular diseases.

Fluorescein Angiography

Retinal vessel imaging for diagnosis of diabetic retinopathy and vascular occlusion.

Intravitreal Injection

Anti-VEGF drug injection for treatment of macular edema and wet AMD.

Visual Field Testing (Perimetry)

Precise visual field assessment for diagnosis and monitoring of glaucoma and optic nerve damage.

Intraocular Pressure Measurement

Accurate measurement of intraocular pressure (tonometry) for early glaucoma detection.
